## Career Options After 12th Science

### Undergraduate Courses

You can pursue undergraduate courses such as B.Sc., B.Tech., or B.E. in various fields like physics, chemistry, biology, computer science, mathematics, and engineering.

### Popular Career Options

1. **Medical Field:**
	* MBBS, BDS, BAMS, BHMS, etc.
	* Careers include doctor, surgeon, dentist, and pharmacists.
2. **Engineering:**
	* Aerospace, Biotechnology, Chemical, Civil, Computer Science, Electrical, Electronics, Mechanical, etc.
	* Careers include software development, data analysis, and IT.
3. **Science Research and Academia:**
	* Careers include scientists, researchers, and professors.
4. **Data Analysis and Science:**
	* Careers include data analysts, data scientists, and business analysts.
5. **Software Development and IT:**
	* Careers include software developers, IT consultants, and system analysts.
6. **Environmental Science and Conservation:**
	* Careers include environmental scientists, conservationists, and sustainability experts.
7. **Biotechnology and Genetic Engineering:**
	* Careers include biotechnologists, genetic engineers, and pharmaceutical researchers.

### Vocational Courses and Certification Programs

1. **Computer Programming:**
	* Careers include software developers, programmers, and coding specialists.
2. **Data Analysis:**
	* Careers include data analysts, data scientists, and business analysts.
3. **Digital Marketing:**
	* Careers include digital marketers, social media managers, and online advertisers.
4. **Emerging Technologies:**
	* Careers include AI and machine learning engineers, cybersecurity experts, and blockchain developers.
